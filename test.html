About US

Formula Student ISEP is a team born in 2022, by a small group of students with a desire to build a racecar capable of competing with the best teams in Europe. In 2024, we were able to go to our first competition, where we gathered the necessary experience to finally achieve our goal. With more members, more sponsors, and more important, more desire to compete, we are ready to tackle the 2025 season.
