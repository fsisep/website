About us

We are a Formula Student Team based in ISEP - Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto, in Portugal. We started this journey in 2022, building a small team with a desire to create a car to compete at the highest level of Formula Student competitions. In 2024, we managed to compete for the first time, in the Class II, where we presented our entire plan and design to the grand jury of Formula Student Portugal. We are now building our first car, looking to finally compete with a physical car in 2025!
